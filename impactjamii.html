About Impact Jamii

Driving sustainable change through youth-led initiatives

Who We Are

Impact Jamii is a forward-looking youth-led Community Based Organization (C.B.O) registered in Kenya under Community Group Registration Act reg No. DSD/30/159/02/17447. We were established by visionary youth and youth leaders with a convergent mind and a goal of tackling critical societal problems which hamper sustainable development of our communities.

Our Focus Areas

After careful examination of common societal problems we choose to focus on the following 6 thematic areas:

  • Sexual and Reproductive Health rights (ending teen pregnancies and period poverty)
  • Mental health advocacy
  • Substance abuse prevention
  • Health care access for underserved communities
  • Youth employment solutions
  • Climate Action

We are persuaded that by addressing these challenges we shall not only prepare our societies but also enable it to actualize real and sustainable transformation today and in the future.

Our Focus Areas

Addressing critical challenges for sustainable development

Sexual & Reproductive Health

Ending teen pregnancies and period poverty for girls in marginalized areas through education and resource distribution.

Mental Health Advocacy

Providing mental health support and resources to communities, reducing stigma and promoting well-being.

Substance Abuse Prevention

Implementing prevention programs and supporting rehabilitation for those affected by substance abuse.

Healthcare Access

Increasing access to quality healthcare services for underserved communities in Baringo County.

Youth Employment

Creating opportunities and pathways for youth employment through skills training and job placement.

Climate Action

Promoting environmental conservation and climate resilience through community-based initiatives.
